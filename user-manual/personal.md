# GARY-PERSONAL(1) — User Manual

## NAME

**gary-personal** — color and background

## SYNOPSIS

```
gary personal [--aviation] [--fdm] [--pinball] [--red-pill] [--skiing]
```

## OPTIONS

**--aviation**
: Aviation hobbyist with a private and instrument rating. Currently working on my comercial and
  flight instructor ratings. I fly a STOL (slow) airplane (Savage Norden) and fast airplane
  (Harmon Rocket) out of Concord airport. These aircraft are experimental and I'm always
  improving them. I've designed, fabricated and installed "glass panel" avionics and autopilots
  on two planes, including my Harmon Rocket.

**--fdm**
: Active 3D printing enthusiast. Design parts in Fusion 360 and print them on a Bambu H2D,
  regularly in engineering materials like PAHT-CF and PPA-CF for aviation use.

**--pinball**
: Active pinball player. Have owned a Data East [Star Wars](https://pinside.com/pinball/machine/star-wars) since 2001; current favorite is
  Stern [Godzilla](https://pinside.com/pinball/machine/godzilla-limited-edition).

**--red-pill**
: I've taken the red pill on spec-driven, agentic development and gone all the way down the
  rabbit hole. I now reach for it well beyond software — debugging aviation squawks, researching
  colleges with my kids, and most other decisions with enough surface area to benefit from a
  real spec.

**--skiing**
: Skier since elementary school. Often found at Palisades, Mammoth, or Kirkwood on a powder
  day. Favorite "sleeper" trail is Tower Three Chute at Jackson Hole.
